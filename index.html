About Us

Established in 2009, PT. Lintang Utama Infotek (LUI) specializes in procuring goods and services, particularly IBM products like servers, storage, and spare parts. Since 2015, we have shifted our focus to providing solution-oriented products and comprehensive maintenance services, including Service Consultation, Virtualization, Back-Up and Recovery Solutions, Network Configurations, Data Protection, and Disaster Recovery.
