About Us

Established in 2009, PT. Lintang Utama Infotek specializes in procuring goods and services, particularly spare parts for IBM Server, Storage, and other IBM products. Since 2015, our focus has shifted to providing comprehensive solutions, unlocking the full potential of Data Centers with expertise in Server, Storage, and Networking Solutions. This includes maintenance, services, spare parts, advanced monitoring, and customized ERP solutions.
